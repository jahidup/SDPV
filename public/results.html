Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my Registration Number?

Your registration number is printed on your admit card and fee receipt. It typically starts with "SDP" followed by a 5‑digit code.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We validate only the registration number and date of birth. No personal data is stored or shared during the check.

The result is not showing up. What should I do?

Ensure you entered the correct registration number and date of birth exactly as per your records. If the problem persists, contact our help desk via WhatsApp.
