About Sankalp Digital Pathshala

Where tradition meets technology – a 15‑year legacy of academic excellence, now supercharged with AI to deliver education that truly transforms lives.

Our Story

Sankalp Digital Pathshala was born in 2010 with a simple yet powerful belief: every student deserves access to premium education, regardless of their background or location.

What started as a small coaching centre with three passionate teachers has grown into a full‑fledged digital learning platform serving over 15,000 students across India. We've embraced technology not to replace teachers, but to amplify their impact – making world‑class mentorship available at every student’s fingertips.

Today, we are proud to be at the forefront of the AI‑powered education revolution, constantly innovating to ensure our learners stay ahead.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratise premium education by integrating artificial intelligence with compassionate teaching, ensuring every student gains the skills, confidence, and curiosity to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
